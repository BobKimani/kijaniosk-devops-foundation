# Cloud Service Model

The KijaniKiosk platform is best suited to a **Platform as a Service (PaaS)** model.

## Justification

PaaS allows the KijaniKiosk engineering team to focus on building and deploying application features without managing underlying infrastructure such as servers and operating systems.

This is important because:
- The platform is still in early development
- The team needs to move quickly and iterate
- Managing infrastructure manually (IaaS) would slow down development

## Why not IaaS?
IaaS would require managing virtual machines, networking, and scaling manually, increasing operational complexity.

## Why not SaaS?
SaaS does not provide enough control or flexibility for building a custom application like KijaniKiosk.

## Conclusion
PaaS provides the best balance between control and simplicity, enabling faster development while maintaining scalability.
